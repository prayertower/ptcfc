Join the Team

At Prayer Tower you can join one of our many teams where you can find your purpose and serve others by using your God-given gifts. If you have a heart for kids or a creative talent; if you love to pray or sing; if you have a burden for the poor or those who do not know Jesus– there is a team for you! If you want to find out more or have questions about a team, come meet the teams:

How do I join a team? If you are a Christian, PTCFC is your home church and you agree with our Statement of Faith, joining a team is easy: Find a team, Sign up, Meet with the team leader, Start serving


Volunteer With Us Through the Following Ministries

Ushering & Security.

With an increasing number of people coming to worship with their family, the need for ushering has highly increased. We need people to help with taking care of children during worship, helping people find appropriate seats during service and general ushering duties. We need people with the natural tender and welcoming hearts to open up and offer to serve in this capacity.

Kids Church.

As the number of worshipers and seekers is increasing so is the number of children. We have realised that ministry to children is as important as ministry to the adults. We are in need of passionate people who have the patience to handle and relate well with children to take the challenge of the children ministry. Children have various needs depending on their age group. We need gifted people who will take care of these needs.

Music Team.

Music ministry is one of the major activity and ministry in church. God commands us to play skillfully. If you have the giftings for any musical instrument, please come on board and be part of the team to usher people in the presence of God. We need people with giftings for directing and co-ordinating voices, sound engineers and so on.

Prayer Team.

One of the pillars of Prayer Tower as the name suggests is prayer and we are well known as a praying church. To keep the fire burning, we need gifted intercessors and prayer worriers who will be willing to carry the burden of prayer not only for the church but for the community, the lost and the nations of the world.

Administrative Support.

A church administrative assistant attends to the many different office and administrative duties of the church. Someone who will serve beneath the church leaders and pastor, attending to the general running of the church office and addressing the church’s short and long term business dealings.

Maintanance Staff

The Maintenance team is responsible for the upkeep of the Church premises and external property, as well as supporting the teams of the church and special projects. Activities of the teams include, but are not limited to cleaning the church premises, packing and unpacking chairs, instruments and other activities

Compassion and Care

Ministry is fulfilling when the felt needs of the people are met. Our church carries out month care and support. We need willing and compassionate people to take up the challenge. Compassion, care, and community for those with special needs; people living with disabilities, the elderly, and the chronically and critically ill etc.

Outreach Team

We need passionate and gifted people who will engage weekly in outreach. The Evangelism Team to goes out into the streets of Colridge and other areas to share the good news of Jesus Christ, give out invitation fliers and to invite people to join us for the Sunday service and other services at Prayer Tower.
