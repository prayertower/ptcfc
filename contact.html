Prayer Tower Christian Fellowship Centre is a ministry that exist to propagate the gospel of our LORD and savior Jesus Christ. As a result, we consider ministry to be priority to us. We have a vision to plant churches across Southern Africa – churches that will plant other churches.

In this regard, our ministry can only be complete with the strength of the man power and resources that the LORD privileges us to put together. If the LORD so impresses upon your heart to be a partner to this ministry please get in touch with us. Remember you become a partaker of the blessings that follows anyone you are connected to; Lot partook of Abrahams blessings, Elisha got double potion of Elijah’s anointing, the disciples and the women who ministered to Jesus with their substance partook of the blessings of Jesus, you can also partake of the blessings and the grace that follows this ministry. Feel free to get in touch with us.
